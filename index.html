Šta je advokat?

Advokat ili advokat je član pravne profesije. Profesija
uključuje sudije, sudije, javne beležnike, pomoćne advokate i
druge koji pružaju pravne usluge javnosti.

Ko reguliše advokate?

The American Bar Association (ABA) oversees standards and
practices for attorneys nationwide. It provides guidance and
model rules for state bar associations to copy for their own
uses.

Koje su dužnosti advokata?

A lawyer has several enumerated duties under the Rules of
Professional Conduct. A client has the right to expect their
attorney to exercise these duties during the course of legal
representation.
